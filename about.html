About Us

Since 2006, Renaissance Property Associates, LLC has managed portfolios of properties that make a difference in the community building a record of success and longevity. Our devoted team is doing the job right, building a presence in the area, and making a difference In the community.

Commercial Properties

We offer a roster of properties for rent in the historic seaside hamlet of Oyster Bay including converted Victorians, in town offices and ground floor retail.

For families, we have a property with a floor plan to suit your lifestyle. If apartment living is your choice, we have apartment rentals in a variety of multi-use properties. We encourage you to view our listings and fill out an application.
